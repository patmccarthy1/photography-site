Ko Samui

In late 2018, I visited the island of Ko Samui in the Gulf of Thailand with my family. I have never been somewhere which exudes such vibrancy yet also such tranquility. You may find yourself under the roof of a busy food market or daydreaming on a paradisiacal beach, but everywhere on Ko Samui you will find colour, beauty and life.
